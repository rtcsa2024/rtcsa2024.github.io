Venue: Lotte Resort Sokcho

RTCSA 2024 will be co-located with NVMSA 2024 at the Lotte Resort Sokcho. Lotte Resort Sokcho, situated along the stunning coastline of the East Sea in Sokcho, South Korea, offers an exceptional destination for leisure and professional gatherings. The resort is renowned for its breathtaking ocean views, available from every room, providing guests a serene and luxurious experience.
