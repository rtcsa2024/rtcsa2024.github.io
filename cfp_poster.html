Call for Posters

The RTCSA 2024 poster session provides an opportunity to present early-stage work and receive feedback from not only the real-time/embedded system community but also the non-volatile memory systems community from NVMSA co-located with the conference. Both research and industry presentations are solicited.

 

Abstract Submission Guidelines

  • Poster abstracts have at most 2 pages of technical content, including all text, figures, tables, appendices, references, etc.
  • The submitted extended abstracts are subject to single-blind reviews.
  • Authors should use an IEEE two-column conference style with 10pt font size, on US Letter paper size according to the IEEE conference paper proceedings format.
