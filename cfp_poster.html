Instructions for Presenters

  • Poster Presentations will be held on August 21 (Wed.) 18:00 - 19:00 in Grand Ballroom (Grand B) on the 1st floor map.
  • Each poster presenter is responsible for printing and bringing their own poster.
  • The organizing committee does not provide an official poster template. Each presenter’s poster must fit an A0-sized poster in portrait mode (33.1’ x 46.8’ or 841mm x 1189mm).
  • Presenters are requested to complete putting up their posters at poster boards before the poster session. Poster can be put up from 9:00 a.m. and thumbtacks and masking tapes will be provided at the conference room.
  • Each poster board at the conference room is assigned a poster number, which is shown in the above list. Each presenter is required to put up his/her poster to the poster board corresponding to his/her poster number.
  • Please make a presentation at each poster location once the session starts.


Call for Posters

The RTCSA 2024 poster session provides an opportunity to present early-stage work and receive feedback from not only the real-time/embedded system community but also the non-volatile memory systems community from NVMSA co-located with the conference. Both research and industry presentations are solicited.

 

Abstract Submission Guidelines

  • Poster abstracts have at most 2 pages of technical content, including all text, figures, tables, appendices, references, etc.
  • The submitted extended abstracts are subject to single-blind reviews.
  • Authors should use an IEEE two-column conference style with 10pt font size, on US Letter paper size according to the IEEE conference paper proceedings format.
  • The accepted abstracts will be published in the RTCSA 2024’s proceedings.
  • Submission web page at:
    • Submission Portal

Topic of Interest

  • The RTCSA 2024 poster session seeks the presentation in the areas described in Call for Papers, including 1) REAL-TIME SYSTEMS TRACK, 2) IoT, CPS, AND EMERGING APPLICATIONS TRACK, and 3) EMBEDDED SYSTEMS TRACK.

  • Acceptance notification: June 28, 2024 → July 5, 2024 (Extended)
  • Camera-ready deadline: July 5, 2024 → July 8, 2024 (Extended)
