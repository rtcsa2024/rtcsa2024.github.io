Notes

  • Each accepted paper must have one author registration completed by one of the authors, regardless of the author’s academic status.
  • Each author registration covers only one accepted paper and is non-refundable.
  • Student rate is applicable only to those who have student status at the time of the symposium.
  • Please be advised that the student rate will not be applied for students who have a full time job or Post-doc.
  • Life member rate is applicable to an IEEE member whose membership status is “Life”.
  • For assistance in registration matters, please contact RTCSA 2024 Registration Chair Hyosu Kim at hskimhello@cau.ac.kr

Cancellation Policy

  • If you have already registered for RTCSA 2024 and find that you are unable to attend or cancel the paper, please notify RTCSA 2024 Registration Chair in writing to hskimhello@cau.ac.kr to request a refund and cancel your registration.
  • Cancellation deadline: August 6, 2024
  • Cancellation fee: 50% of the registration fee
  • All refunds will be made after the symposium.
